# Docere Foundation — Awareness Video Script
**"Impacting Communities for Jesus Christ"**
Full Mission Overview | 60–90 seconds | Voiceover + On-Screen Text

---

## Production Notes
- **Tone:** Warm, urgent, hopeful. Not a charity plea — a bold declaration.
- **Voiceover style:** Calm but passionate. Conversational, not corporate. Think a trusted friend who deeply believes in this cause.
- **Music:** Begin with soft, ambient African instrumentation (e.g., light thumb piano / kora). Swell gently from Scene 3 onward. Peak emotional volume at Scene 5. Fade under CTA.
- **Subtitles:** Always on. White text with subtle dark drop shadow for readability on all backgrounds.
- **Pacing note:** Pauses marked with [beat] — these are intentional. Let them breathe.

---

## SCENE 1 — The Hook (0:00 – 0:05)
**Visuals:** Aerial shot of a rural African village at dawn. Golden light. Children walking a dirt road. Bare feet. No voiceover.

**ON-SCREEN TEXT (bold, white, centred):**
> *"Millions of people across Africa are still waiting for someone to show up."*
